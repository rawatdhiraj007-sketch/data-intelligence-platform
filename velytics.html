FAQ

Common questions

Do I need any technical skills? ▼
Absolutely not. If you can upload a file, you can use Velytics. No SQL, no Python, no data science knowledge required. We built it specifically for business owners, managers, and teams who want answers — not tools.
Is my data safe? ▼
Yes. Your data is processed in memory and deleted immediately after analysis. We never store, share, or use your data for any purpose. No database, no cloud storage, no logs. Your file stays yours.
What file formats does Velytics accept? ▼
We accept Excel (.xlsx, .xls) and CSV files. Multi-sheet Excel files are supported — you can pick which sheet to analyse. File size up to 200MB. If your file is messy, that's fine — our Smart Clean engine handles it.
What if my Excel file is messy or has multiple headers? ▼
That's exactly what Velytics is built for. Our Smart Clean engine automatically detects the real header row, removes title rows, converts text-numbers, standardises dates, and removes garbage. 23 types of Excel problems are fixed automatically before analysis begins.
How is Velytics different from Power BI or Tableau? ▼
Power BI and Tableau are powerful but require setup, training, and clean data. Velytics requires nothing — upload and get insights in 60 seconds. We also auto-clean your data, which BI tools cannot do. And we're 90% cheaper.
Can I cancel my subscription anytime? ▼
Yes, cancel anytime with one click. No contracts, no cancellation fees, no questions asked. You can also start with the free plan and upgrade only when you need more uploads.
Which industries does Velytics support? ▼
Currently live: Sales, Inventory, HR & Payroll, Finance & Accounting. Coming soon: Retail, Logistics, Healthcare, Restaurant, Manufacturing, Marketing, Education, Hospitality, Agriculture, and more — 20 industries total by end of year.
Do you offer a free trial? ▼
Yes — our Starter plan is free forever with 5 uploads per month. No credit card needed. Just sign up and upload your first file. You'll see results in under 60 seconds.
